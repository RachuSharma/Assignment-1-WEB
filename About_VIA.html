VIA


    Via University Horsens in Denmark is known for its English-taught programs, providing students with a global perspective
    and practical skills. Located in the charming city of Horsens, it offers quality education in a diverse, modern setting, making
    it a top choice for those seeking an English-language higher education in Denmark, with a wide range of professional degree
    programs.


    Via University Horsens in Denmark offers a range of professional degree programs, including a notable emphasis on Software Engineering. These programs prioritize practical knowledge and hands-on experience, ensuring that students are well-prepared
    for the demands of the industry. Collaborative teamwork is a core component, with group projects fostering valuable skills in communication and problem-solving, making it an ideal choice for those looking to excel in the field of Software Engineering
    through a hands-on, practical approach. Located in the picturesque city of Horsens, Via University provides an excellent
    environment for students to thrive academically and professionally.
